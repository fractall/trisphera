Project Scope

Client

Thync

Area

Startup

Services

Branding, Design

Thync is a wearable device worn on your temple and controlled through your smartphone. After selecting a ‘vibe’ from the app, the device sends small electric waveforms to activate specific neural pathways that make you feel more “calm” or “energized.
We wanted to brand the website accordingly to the device itself, in a stylish and modern way, thus clear, light colors were chosen.
